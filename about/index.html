About author

Beginning of the journey

2018 is the year that I first stepped into this industry.
After couples of months of self education, I got basic knowledges of web frontend
development, at the moment, I think I was prepare enough to find a job.
What I’ve known then was just html, css, jquery essentials,
Luckily I found a job, and my career began.

A chance to do more

I started from learning how to style a web page using bootstrap, make http requests, operate with the dom by jquery selector, etc…
I was cooperating with a backend developer until he quit the job, thus backend also became one of my duty.
As a junior web developer, it’s hard for me at the first time to act like a fullstack, but I feel joyful everytime when I achieved something, even if it’s as simple as adding a record into mysql database, that was the main reason to keep me alive.
Now I’m glad I could have such experience which grew me up.

Gaol of writing

The more I dig into it, the bigger I realized this world is. Backend, Frontend, Linux, Nginx, React, Angular, blablabla… there are so much stuffs to learn, I know I will never reach the end, but at least I can record something important on the way of being a better developer.
The main goal I start writing is to remind myself what I’ve been walked through, it will cover many aspects indeed, and the biggest motivation which encourages me to writie is - every developer should have his/her own tech blog.
