Beginning of the journey

2018 is the year that I first stepped into this industry.
After couples of months of self education, I got basic knowledges of web frontend
development, at the moment, I think I was prepare enough to find a job.
What i’ve known then was just html, css, jquery essentials,
Luckily I found a job, and my career began.

Find the interest

I started from learning how to style using bootstrap, make http requests, operate with the dom by jquery selector, etc…
I was cooperating with a backend developer until he quit the job, thus backend also became one of my duty.
As a junior web developer, it’s hard for me at the first time, but I feel joyful everytime I achieved something, even if it’s as simple as adding a record into mysql database.

Gaol of writing
